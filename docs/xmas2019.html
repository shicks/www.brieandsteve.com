Hicks family matchy-matchy

2019 has been another full year for the Hicks Family. We have many things to be thankful for.

Cubbie and Reader

Cardiffs “All Aboard”


Brianna still works part time at Cornerstone Research. She enjoys managing and appreciates the wonderful firm culture. She had business trips to Austin, TX, and Boston, MA, this year.

Steve still works at Google. Outside of work he dabbles in retro video game hacking and streaming, and built a randomizer for the classic NES game Crystalis, spawning a fledgling community.

It's a small world, but a long day

Now that both children are a little older, Brie and Steve have resumed serving in children's ministry at church, teaching the middle-elementary group once a month, and have finally found a new community group to plug into.

Buddies!


Brianna dusted off her piano playing skills to perform a duet with her sister Katherine at their Grandmother's memorial service. It's nice to get away

Brie and Steve celebrated their 7th Wedding Anniversary with a weekend trip to Las Vegas (the longest they had been apart from the kids since becoming parents!).

Blessings,
Brianna, Steve, Christopher, and Una
